tic tac toe
-------------------
9 grids  a 3 by 3 grid

2 input types (norts or crosses)

2 players who are assigned either a nort or a cross (which will remain the same throughout the game)

To begin the game PLAYER 1 chooses a unique grid to assign nort or cross (only one nort or cross per grid)

PLAYER 2 chooses a grid to place their nort or cross (must be different grid)

if a player places 3 of their assigned nort or cross horizontally, diagonally or  vertically = win

if no winner game draws
